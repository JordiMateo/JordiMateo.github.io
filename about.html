Currently, I am a lecturer at the University of Lleida (UdL). I am a teacher in the Grau en Tècniques d'Interacció Digital i de Computació (GTIDIC) at Igualada Campus . I belong to the Computer Science Department (DIEI) and concretely to the Distributed Computation Group (GCD). I received the B.Sc and M.S in computer engineering degrees from the Escola Politècnica Superior (EPS) of the Universitat de Lleida (UdL) in 2012 AND 2013, respectively. I hold a Ph.D., Cum Laude, in Computer Science / Cloud services by the University of Lleida(UdL, 2019).

Lecturing: Currently, I am coordinating four courses: Innovation in ICT, Development of Mobile Devices Applications, Systems Administration and Virtualization, and Operating Systems. I have lectured different courses in different Computer/Data Science topics. My principal teaching area is Operating Systems. I have lectured 3 years (2015-2016, 2016-2017, 207-2018) as a teacher assistant in the Grau en Enginyeria Informàtica at the University of Lleida. Nowadays, I am coordinating the Operating System course in the "Grau en Tècniques d'Interacció Digital i de Computació" at Igualada Campus. I have also experience teaching in topics related to High Performace Computing, such as MPI or OpenMP. I taught the High-Performance Computing course in the Master of Computer Science (UdL, 2018-2019). I taught two sessions of a post-graduate course (CyberSecurity, 2019, UdL), a session about security at the Operating System level and security in the cloud.

Research: My main research interest is Cloud architectures and services. Furthermore, I am also interested in High-Performance Computing, eHealth, Agriculture 4.0, Decision Support Systems and IoT platforms. I have published 22 journal papers in the Science Citation Index. Currently, I am supervising a Ph.D. related to data analysis. This thesis is aimed at studying correlations and patterns in cancer detection.
